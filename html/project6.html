LOW-KI: Sweden’s Vegan Protein Cereal

Posted: December 10, 2024

Last Edited: February 18, 2025

Project Overview

This project involves co-founding LOW-KI, Sweden’s first vegan and gluten-free cereal, and designing its e-commerce platform on Shopify. LOW-KI offers high-protein cereal pops made from non-GMO soy protein isolate, with 30g of protein per 100g, low sugar, and natural, home-baked ingredients. Launched as a health-conscious option for breakfast, snacks, or coffee breaks, it caters to vegan, gluten-free, and lactose-free diets. The site, live since its inception, features free shipping over 299 SEK, 2-3 day delivery, and emphasizes Swedish production, driving strong customer engagement via social media (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube).

Key Features

  • Product: Vegan, gluten-free, high-protein cereal (30g/100g)
  • Target Market: Health-conscious consumers, dietary restrictions
  • E-commerce: Shopify platform, www.lowki.se, with fast delivery and free shipping
  • Engagement: Social media presence for marketing and community building
