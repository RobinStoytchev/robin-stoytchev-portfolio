Comps Valuation: Ratos B

Posted: December 10, 2024

Last Edited: February 18, 2025

Project Overview

This project conducts a Comparable Companies (Comps) valuation of Ratos B, implying a target EV/EBITDA multiple of 9.3x as of August 4, 2024. Please note that this is meant as showcase of previous work, and numbers are not up to date. The analysis benchmarks Ratos B against peers like Bure Equity, Creades, and Svolder, with an average peer EV/EBITDA multiple of 6.7x. Ratos B’s current EV/EBITDA of 7.2x suggests it is undervalued, offering a 39% upside based on the implied share price of SEK 132.20, compared to the current price of SEK 34.18.

Key Valuation Metrics

  • Target EV/EBITDA Multiple: 9.3x
  • Peer Group Average EV/EBITDA: 6.7x
  • Ratos B EV/EBITDA: 7.2x
  • Implied Share Price: SEK 132.20

The analysis uses revenue, EBITDA, and net income data, adjusted for sales CAGR (3.0%) and EBITDA margins (5.5%). Sensitivity to peer multiples and industry norms underscores potential risks and opportunities. Access the full report for detailed peer comparisons, assumptions, and methodology.
