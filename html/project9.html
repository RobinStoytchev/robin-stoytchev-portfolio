Coca-Cola Brand Colors

In May of 2025, I created a presentation for Coca-Cola Europacific Partners. Underpinning the presentation was a business case for Q1 Performance, containing several data sets. Mainly, I utilized Power BI to first manipulate and transform the data into a suitable format for data analysis. This involved creating custom metrics and table columns. Then, I could showcase the visual graphs and tables. On this page, I therefore want to highlight and visually represent the awesome brand color pallette that Coca-Cola EP has and that I got to work with! The presentation itself in Non-Disclosure.
